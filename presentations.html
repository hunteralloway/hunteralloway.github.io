    A Magnetic Induction Communication Link

    I had the privaledge of working with research intern Hailey Dowling, and postdoctoral research assistant Dr. Parisa Rangraz at the UWSTREAM laboratory, to test a magnetic induction link. The link utilizes two mutually coupled coils in the near-field that pass a signal generated by an arbitrary waveform generator, to transmit information wirelessely.

    Our lab had the opportunity to show-case this technology to Minister Colton LeBlanc, which was a result of the government of Nova Scotia's investing $1.4 Million towards microelectronics research at Dalhousie University.
